Checking spell ranks in Vanilla WoW

I wanted to improve an existing addon for mouse-over spell casting by reducing some of the repeated code and by adding smart buff down-ranking. This second feature proved to be a little interesting.

Checking for buffs and spells outside of the combat log is completely unintuitive task in Vanilla WoW. You can’t call some function to get all the information about a spell by it’s ID or name. You want to know if a player knows a spell? You have to crawl the spellbook to see if there’s a match for what you’re looking for. But what exactly is there to match? A spell name? No, you get to check paths of textures.

Call GetSpellTexture with the index of a spell in the spellbook to get spell’s icon texture path, then check check it against what you’re looking for.

As far as I have been able to tell, this is the most trivial way to scan one’s spell book currently. This technique is also the same for checking buffs and debuffs without scanning for aura gains and fades in the combat log. What a pain.
