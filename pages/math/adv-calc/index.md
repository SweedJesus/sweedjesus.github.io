## Continuity of functions <!-- 5 -->

### Uniform continuity <!-- 5.3 -->

A function $f$ is *uniformly continuous on an interval $D\subset\R$* if and only if:

$$
\forall\epsilon>0,
\exists\delta>0,
\forall x_1,x_2\in D,
|x_2-x_1|<\delta \Rightarrow
|f(x_2)-f(x_1)|<\epsilon
$$
