% 1
For \(f(x,y)=3x^4y^2-x\cos y+4x^3y^3\), find \(f_{x}\), \(f_{y}\),
\(f_{xx}\) and \(f_{xy}\).
===
\(\begin{aligned}[t]
  f_{x}&=12x^3y^2-\cos y+12x^2y^3\\
  f_{y}&=6x^4y+x\sin y+12x^3y^2\\
  f_{xx}&=36x^2y^2+24xy^3\\
  f_{yy}&=6x^4+x\cos y+24x^3y\\
  f_{xy}&=24x^3y+\sin y+36x^2y^2
\end{aligned}\)
